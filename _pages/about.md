## About Me

I am a second-year master‘s student at Guangdong University of Foreign Studies, with a research focus on the security of large language models, specifically on jailbreak attacks—their mechanisms and potential defenses. My current work explores how LLMs can be exploited through malicious prompts and seeks to develop more robust alignment strategies to mitigate such vulnerabilities. As I am still in the early stages of my academic journey, I am actively investigating cutting-edge issues in this field and hope to contribute to building more secure and trustworthy AI systems in the future.
